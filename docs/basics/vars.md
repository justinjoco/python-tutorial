## Operators

### Arithmetic
- Addition: `+`
- Subtraction: `-`
- Multiplication: `*`
- Division: `/`
- Floor (Integer) Division `//`
- Modulo `%`
- Exponent `**`


### Logical 
- Less than `<`
- Less than or equal to `<=`
- Equal to `==`
- Greater than or equal to `>=`
- Greater than `>`
- Not equal `!=`

### Assignment operators

- Add, then assign: `+=`
- Subtract, then assign `-=`
- Multiply, then assign `*=`
- Divide, then assign `/=`
- Modulo, then assign: `%=`

### Bitwise

- Bitwise AND `&`
- Bitwise OR `|`
- Bitwise XOR `^`
- Bitwise NOT `~`
- Bitwise left shift `<<`
- Bitwise right shift `>>`
